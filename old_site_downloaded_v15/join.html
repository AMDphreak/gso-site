Play with the GSO

The Germantown Symphony is an all-volunteer community orchestra made up of musicians from all over the Mid-South. We are a diverse group, but we are united by our love of music. If you are passionate about playing orchestral music and want to join a group that is supportive and fun, please get in touch! 

We play several classical concerts each season that feature members of the orchestra, soloists, and young artists, plus our ever-popular holiday concert. During the summer, we perform a series of pops concerts.  We rehearse on Thursday evenings from 7:30-9:30pm.
